MINESWEEPER






INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to minesweeper. If you're a windows user chances are that
you already have the game on your computer. This guide will help you
in completing your first game. The rule of the game is simple, the
number on a block shows the number of mines adjacent to it and you've
to find out all the mines.

Right click on any box to put a flag on it when you're confirmed
that there is a mine. Click the smiley face to restart the game. The
upper left box shows the number of flags you placed and the right box
is the timer which gives you 2000 seconds to finish the game.

Click the boxes to reveal. If there are no mines in the neighbouring
boxes or in that box, the boxes will get cleared up. Clicking on a
mined box will cause you to lose the game. Find out the mined boxes
and reveal all the rest ones to win the game.
