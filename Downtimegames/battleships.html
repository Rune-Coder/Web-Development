Battleships






Instructions

Battleship is strategy type guessing game for two players.
It is played on ruled grids on which each player's fleet of
ships are marked. The locations of the fleet are concealed
from the other player. Players alternate turns calling "shots"
at the other player's ships, and the objective of the game
is to destroy the opposing player's fleet.


The game is played on two grids, one for each player.
The grids are square of size 10 x 10. Each ship occupies
a particular number of consecutive squares on the grid,
arranged either horizontally or vertically. The number of
squares for each ships is determined by the type of ship.
The ships cannot overlap i.e only one ship can occupy any
given square in the grid. The types and number of ships allowed
are the same for each player. After hitting all the squares of a
particular ship, it is sunk. Sink all the five ships of the opposing
player to win.


The rules specify the following ships.


Aircraft Carrier - 5 squares
Battleship - 4 squares
Submarine - 3 squares
Cruiser - 3 squares
Destroyer - 2 squares


How to play

Place the ships correctly on your board. Choose the type of
ship to be placed from the ship palette. Then click the consecutive
squares to place the particular ship.Click the "ready" button to start
the game. Your opponent's board will open and you click on your desired
square. It willl be followed by the enemy's turn. Alternately take your
shots before one wins the game.
